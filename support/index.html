Commercial

How do I buy?
You have 3 options:
  • Directly from the developers, who will ♥ you, on this website (the red Buy buttons, when available).
  • Through Adobe Add-ons, the official Adobe web based app store, for Photoshop, After Efefcts and Premiere from CC to CC2015.5 (the blue Buy buttons). CS6 relies on the Adobe Exchange panel).
  • Through AE Scripts, the which market our After Effects, Premiere and Photoshop products
How can I pay?
The e-commerce provider for both this website and Adobe Exchange is Fastspring; it manages Visa®, Mastercard®, American Express®, Discover®, JCB®, PayPal, bank transfer, check, money order, and purchase order. Payments are accepted in ten major currencies in addition to the US Dollar.
Can I install your products in all of my computers?
This is a tough one. We don't enforce it, yet we are OK with up to 3 computers
What's your upgrade policy?
Fixel Algorithms products version numbering is as following: <MajorVersion>.<MinorVersion>.<BuildNumber>. Product owners are entitled to free minor version and build number upgrades.
Adobe Exchange is CC / CS6 only, I need CS5
Some of our products support CS5 too (Sometimes with limited UI). Contact Us for details.
Do you provide any support?
You’re going to get a lifetime e-mail support! More than 99% of support requests ends successfully. Documentation is found within each product's page.
